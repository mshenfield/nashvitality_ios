This is a new city initiative to recognize and encourage places of work in three areas that contribute to a high quality of life: being green, healthy and involved in the community. Participants can sign up for the challenge at www.mayorsworkplacechallenge.com.

Once signed up, you can complete an online scorecard that includes questions about workplace policies and practices as it relates to being green, healthy and promoting volunteerism. Based on a 100-point scale, organizations can achieve bronze, silver or gold status and can continually update their scorecards over the next 12 months to improve their scores.
